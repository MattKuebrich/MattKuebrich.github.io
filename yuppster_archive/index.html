Introduction

Hello! This is an mp3 archive of all my electronic music from early 1999 to the present. I've chosen to include pretty much everything I've ever made. This includes some really horrible material from when I was first starting out, and some really good stuff that I've never released.

I plan on updating this site as I dig up more old songs, as well as make new ones. You'll notice the little icon next to certain albums/songs. If you click on that icon, you'll be given further information about the tracks.

If you've got comments or questions about the site or my music, you can email me at matt@flatalbert.com.
