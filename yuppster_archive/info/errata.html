Errata

After buying the Frog Peak Collaborations Project, I decided to make my own experimental "1-minute piece based on a recording of Chris Mann's 1-minute text on the nature of collaboration". I named this "errata" because the Frog Peak CD had it's own "errata" insert.

I was turned on to the album because it included electronic music pioneer, Herbert Brun, whom my wife had as a teacher at the University of Illinois. I interviewed Herbert for a paper I wrote in high school on electronic music.
