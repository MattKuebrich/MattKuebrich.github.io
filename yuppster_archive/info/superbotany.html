Super Botany Remix

Released on: Marumari: The Remixes (Carpark, 2002)

The original version of my remix was included with the mp3 supplement to Marumari's remix album. When I made the song, Marumari urged me to consider adding some "backwards guitar" to it. I tried my best, and this is the result. I decide to submit the original version for the album, but since this one has grown on me and I can consider it the superior song.
